ANITA 2023 workshop and `Astroparticle physics & extreme transients' summer school

The 17th annual Australian National Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics science workshop will be held on 8th-10th of February 2023 at the University of Adelaide. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists. Read more about ANITA here. As always, early career researchers are particularly encouraged to contribute talks.

The ANITA summer school on "Astroparticle Physics & Extreme Transients" will be held on 6th-7th of February 2023.

The ANITA workshop and school this year have been developed in cooperation with the ASA/AIP Group for Astroparticle Physics (GAP).

ANITA Workshop 2023

The 2023 ANITA science workshop will be held on the 8th-10th of February at the University of Adelaide.

ANITA 2023 school

The 2023 ANITA summer school will run 6th-7th February at the University of Adelaide.

The theme is Astroparticle physics & extreme transients.
